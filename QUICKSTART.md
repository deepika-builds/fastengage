## Tips for Best Results

### Customize Your Voice (Optional but Recommended)

1. Go to Settings (‚öôÔ∏è)
2. Scroll to "Voice Rules"
3. Add rules like:
   - "Start with the insight"
   - "Use short sentences"
   - "No buzzwords"
4. Scroll to "Example Replies"
5. Paste 5-10 of your best social media replies
6. Click "Save Settings"

### Use Intent Selection

For even better results, select the intent before generating:
- **Auto**: Let AI choose (default)
- **Agree**: When you want to support the idea
- **Disagree**: When you have a different view
- **Add Nuance**: When something's missing
- **Ask Question**: To spark discussion
- **Contrarian**: For a spicy take
- **Add Example**: To provide a case study

### Adjust Boldness

Use the boldness slider:
- **1-3**: Safe and professional
- **4-6**: Confident (default)
- **7-10**: Spicy and attention-grabbing

## Troubleshooting

**"Draft" button not showing?**
- Refresh the page
- Make sure you're on a supported platform
- Check that the extension is enabled

**"API key not configured" error?**
- Go to Settings and add your OpenAI API key
- Make sure you clicked "Save Settings"

**Generation is slow?**
- Normal range is 3-5 seconds
- Check your internet connection
- OpenAI API might be experiencing high traffic

**Replies sound generic?**
- Add more voice rules in Settings
- Add more example replies
- Increase the boldness level
- Make your authority themes more specific
